Social Distancing Activities

Low-risk Activities

Information from Mayo Clinic

Get moving with these low-risk outdoor activities during the pandemic:

  • Walking, running, and hiking
  • Golfing
  • Kayaking, boating, and sailing
  • Picnics
  • Drive-in movies

Zoom Activities

  • Hold a cooking competition with friends or family over Zoom.
  • Get to know your friends and family by playing 20 Questions on Zoom.
  • Start a book club.
  • Host a virtual trivia night.
  • Charades is a fun game that you can easily play on Zoom.
  • Host a virtual movie party.
  • Make a podcast.
  • Challenge your friends or family to a zoom scavenger hunt.
  • Tak an imaginary vacation using Google Earth's Voyager feature.
  • Zoom bingo!
